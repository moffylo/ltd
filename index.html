SERVICES

We supply, and distribute a wide range of high-quality animal feeds to farmers, livestock owners, and agricultural businesses.

Our animal feed flours are carefully selected to meet the nutritional needs of various livestock, providing a cost-effective and sustainable solution for farmers and livestock owners.

We specialize in the distribution of surplus or imperfect spaghetti and pasta products from manufacturers, wholesalers, and retailers.

We specialize in the disassembling of end-of-life vessels, including ships, boats, and offshore structures, in a safe and environmentally responsible manner.

Fabricate and Assemble metal structures, components, and systems using various welding techniques and processes.

Our service involve the fabrication and installation of stainless steel fittings and components for various industrial, commercial, and residential applications.

The availability of various oil like Engine Oils, Transmission Oils, Hydraulic Oil, Gear Oils, Compressor Oils, Turbine Oils, Slideway Oils, Rolling Mill Oils, Circulating Oils, Quenching Oils, Gas Engines Oils, Metalworking Fluids and Greases.
