MOFFYLO LIMITED

We specialize in the disassembling of end-of-life vessels, including ships, boats, and offshore structures, as well as trucks, in a safe and environmentally responsible manner. Our dismantling process ensures that materials are salvaged and repurposed whenever possible, minimizing environmental impact.

We operate in both Warri and Lagos (Breakthrough Vessel CMS Water TAPABAY), adhering to the highest safety standards to ensure proper handling of hazardous materials. Every component is processed in strict compliance with regulatory guidelines, minimizing waste and promoting the recycling of valuable materials. Our efforts actively support sustainability in the marine and transportation industries while maintaining environmental responsibility.
